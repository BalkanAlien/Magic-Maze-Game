To start the game select the number of players (1-4) and the number of cards per player (1 - 24/number of players) after that click the start new game button to initialize a new game.



During the game each player has to find their revealed treasure card. The player tries to get to the room showing the same treasure as on this card. Each turn the player has to slide the rooms by clicking the respective arrow button, after that the player will be able to move their playing piece

Sliding

The player inserts the extra room into the game board where one of the arrows shows the slidable rows and columns, until another room is pushed out of the maze on the opposite side.

Extra rules

  • The room cannot be inserted back into the board at the same place where it was pushed out.

  • Player can rollback after moving their playing piece

  • If the room you push out has a playing piece on it, put this piece on the opposite side of the board on the room that was just placed.

  • Treasures always move with the rooms.The game is over as soon as a player has found all their treasure cards and returned their playing piece to its starting position.

Game Over

The first player to reach all their treasures and go back to their initial position wins the game.
